## DEMO.md script (~3 min)
1. **Problem** (30 s): 91% learning poverty; teachers can't diagnose 40 kids 1:1
2. **Child** (90 s): avatar tap → 4–5 items, answer 2 wrong ON PURPOSE — point
   at the level dots dropping: "it's finding her true level" → **airplane mode
   ON, answer 2 more** — "school Wi-Fi died; she didn't notice" → airplane off →
   speaking → celebration
3. **Parent** (30 s): result card in Filipino, replay the child's clip
4. **Teacher** (30 s): her row live in the class table next to 12 classmates,
   red flags → "one assessment, three audiences, zero teacher hours"

## Definition of done
Mock path bulletproof on the deployed URL; live mic works on your phone; parent
card renders from a real session; backup video recorded; presenter briefed.

## Do NOT
Build forced alignment, per-phoneme scoring, retry-recording flows, or waveform
visualizations. The transcript match % is the demo.
